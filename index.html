Kijani Premium Honey

This honey is harvested at the peak of the hills in Asilong, West Pokot County. The bees mainly collect nectar from medicinal herbs like Panyrath and Kiptarat in Pokot dialect. The quality of this honey can be compared to Manuka honey from the hills of Nepal.

Pure Natural Honey

Experience the pure, unadulterated taste of our natural honey, straight from the hive to your table.

Discover the delightful world of Kijani Honey's flavored honey collection. Each jar is infused with natural flavors that complement the rich, golden sweetness of our premium honey. Perfect for adding a unique twist to your favorite recipes or enjoying straight from the spoon, our flavored honeys are a treat for the senses.

Lemon Honey: Bright and zesty, our lemon-flavored honey brings a refreshing tang to everything from teas to marinades. It's a fantastic way to add a burst of citrusy goodness to your dishes and beverages.

Flavored Honey

Try our delicious flavored honey varieties, perfect for any occasion. From cinnamon to lemon, we have something for everyone.
