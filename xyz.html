Kijani Premium Honey

This honey is harvested at the peak of the hills in Asilong, West Pokot County. The bees mainly collect nectar from medicinal herbs like Panyrath and Kiptarat in Pokot dialect. The quality of this honey can be compared to Manuka honey from the hills of Nepal.
