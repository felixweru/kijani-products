Kijani Honey Products
Kijani Premium Honey
This is honey harvested at the peak of the hills in Asilong West Pokot County. The bees mainly collect nectar from medicinal herbs like Panyrath and Kiptarat in Pokot dialect. The quality of this honey can only be compared to Manuka honey from the hills of Nepal.
Kijani Keepers Honey
This is a category of honey meant to uplift the small scale apiarists in our country. It is collected from different apiculture farmers, tested, then blended together. It is characteristically dark in color and very sweet. It is loved by many households.
Kijani Select Honey
This honey is harvested from a specific region. It could be Baringo, West Pokot, Nyeri, Tharaka Nithi, Kajiado, etc. It is not mixed at all but rather packed specifically. It is normally amber in color and the level of sweetness depends on the area harvested.
Kijani Highland Honey
This is honey harvested in highly forested areas of the Congo and Uganda border. It is characteristically dark in color and not as sweet as Acacia honey. It is regarded as highly medicinal.
Kijani Lemon Ginger Honey
This is Acacia honey blended with natural Ginger and Lemon using specific quantitative measurements to bring out a unique fine taste. This type of honey helps manage colds and is also used as a sweetener in alcoholic drinks like whisky. Enjoy in Dawa, tea, and smoothies.
Kijani Ginger Honey
This is Acacia honey blended with natural Ginger using specific quantitative measurements to bring out a fine taste. This type of honey helps manage colds and is suitable for individuals who are allergic to lemon.
Kijani Cinnamon Honey
This is Acacia honey blended with natural Cinnamon using specific quantitative measurements to bring out a peculiar taste. This type of honey helps improve digestion and manage stomach upsets.
Kijani Acacia Honey
This is honey harvested in arid and semi-arid areas. It is normally light amber in color and very sweet. It is loved by many people because it is applicable in many household activities as a sweetener.
Kijani Mint Honey
This is Acacia honey specifically blended with mint flavor to bring out a perfect unique taste. It is good for mouth freshening just before beginning your day. It also helps manage colds and leaves one with a great refreshing feeling.
